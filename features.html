FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does the AI analyze security data?

The AI uses deep learning algorithms to detect unusual patterns in video footage and audio levels. It can identify anomalies like unauthorized movements, unusual sounds, or breaches in sensitive zones.

2. How secure is my data?

Our system employs high-level encryption protocols to ensure that all video, audio, and sensor data are securely transmitted and stored. Your privacy is our top priority.

3. What devices are supported for webcam streaming?

The system supports most modern browsers and webcams. For the best experience, ensure you're using a browser with WebRTC support, like Google Chrome or Firefox.
